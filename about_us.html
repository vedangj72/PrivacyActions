About Player

Discover the advanced features and functionality of our offline player app.

Key Features

  • Offline Player: Enjoy a seamless experience by loading all your data offline.
  • Modern Player Features: Includes Picture-in-Picture (PiP), brightness control, and volume double-tap gestures for easy media control.
  • Folder Structure Search: Easily navigate and search through your media with a folder structure feature.
  • Playback Speed: Control the speed of playback to suit your preferences.
  • Audio and Subtitles: Support for custom audio tracks and subtitles for a better viewing experience.
  • Repeat & Lock Mode: Features like repeat mode and screen lock to enhance your experience when needed.

Experience the Future of Media Playback

Our app is designed to provide you with a smooth and intuitive experience, packed with features that allow for complete control over your media. Whether you're listening to music or watching videos, you'll enjoy customizable controls, accessibility options, and more.
