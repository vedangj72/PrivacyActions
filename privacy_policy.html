Privacy Policy Overview

My Player ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our offline video player application (the "My Player"). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

File System Access

The App requests access to your device's file system to display and play video files stored locally on your device. No video files or file metadata are collected, transmitted, or stored by us.

Crash Reports

The App integrates Firebase Crashlytics to collect error logs and crash data. This helps us improve the App's performance. Crashlytics may collect:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, OS version).
  • Application state at the time of the crash.
  • Unique identifiers (e.g., Device ID).

This data is anonymized and used solely for diagnostic purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core functionality, such as browsing and playing your video files.
  • Analyze crash reports to improve performance and user experience.
  • Ensure compliance with legal requirements.
